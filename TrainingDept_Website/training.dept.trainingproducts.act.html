Test Overview

The ACT is one of two major American college entrance exams, which highschool students must take as part of the college application process.


Test Structure

  • English: 75 Questions, 45 Minutes
  • Math: 60 Questions, 60 Minutes
  • Science: 40 Questions, 35 Minutes
  • Option Writing: 1 Prompt, 40 Minutes

Essential Test Taking Skills

Fast reading speed, large english vocabulary, basic mathematic skill set, strong time management, graph reading ability.
