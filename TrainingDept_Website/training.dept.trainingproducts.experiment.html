  • ACT

    Test Overview

    The ACT is one of two major American college entrance exams, which highschool students must take as part of the college application process.

    Students Profiles

    Generally 10th and 11th grade highschool students.

    Concepts Tested

    English, mathematics, reading, science, academic essay writing (optional).

  • SAT

    Test Overview

    The SAT is one of two major American college entrance exams, which highschool students must take as part of the college application process.

    Target Students

    Generally 10th and 11th grade highschool students.

    Concepts Tested

    English, mathematics, reading, academic essay writing (optional)

    Score Range

    200–800 (in 10-point increments) on the combined critical reading and writing section and 200–800 (in 10-point increments) on math two sections. (total 400–1600).

  • TOEFL

    Test Overview

    Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) is a standardized test to measure the English language ability of non-native speakers wishing to enroll in English-speaking universities. The test is accepted by many English-speaking academic and professional institutions.

    Target Students

    Non-native English speakers wishing to enroll in English-speaking universities and or professional organizations.

    Concepts Tested

    Reading, listening, speaking and writing of the English language.

    Score Range

    0 to 30 (in 1 point increments) on each of the 4 sections. So total of 0 to 120.

  • IELTS>

    Test Overview

    The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is an international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers.

    Target Students

    Non-native English speakers wishing to enroll in English-speaking universities and or professional organizations, and any non-native speaker needing to demonstrate their English language proficiency.

    Concepts Tested

    Listening, reading, writing and speaking of the English language.

    Score Range

    0 to 9, in 0.5 band increments.
